# What *is* Hack Club?
The superfical explanation is that "Hack Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in 2014 by Zach Latta to empower highschoolers interested in software and hardware with teaching material and developer tools."

On a deeper level, Hack Club is a Place of Many Things<sup>tm</sup>. Hack Club is a place to meet other developers. Hack Club is a place to take part in silly events with Minecraft and Gartic phone and in-person hackathons and get prizes with You ship, We ship events. 
Hack Club is *so* much more than *just* a nonprofit. Chris Walker wrote an [essay](https://essays.hackclub.com/what-is-hack-club/) about what Hack Club is on a deeper level in August of 2024.

The goal of Hack Club is to produce tinkerers and hackers and leaders. At Hack Club, *everything* is ran by teens.
