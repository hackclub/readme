# Developer tools
## <Emoji name="nest"/> [Nest](https://hackclub.app/)
Slack channel: [#nest](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C056WDR3MQR)

Meet Nest! A 48-core server with 256 gigs of DDR5 and 1.92 terabytes of storage in the form of 2 NVMe drives hosted in Helsinki, Finland, on Hetzner accessable to *any* Hack Club member.
Nest can be used to host websites, or game servers, or backends for projects. 

Check out all the projects on Nest [here](https://hackclub.app/projects)!

## [Spaces](https://spaces.hackclub.com/)
Slack channel: [#spaces](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C08D0UDBSLX)

Virtual development environments for Hack Clubbers. VSCode, Blender, KiCad, and more, in the cloud.

## [Hack Club AI](https://ai.hackclub.com/)
Slack channel: [#hackclub-ai](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C099S1LLFFU)

Free LLM inference ($6 daily limit) for Hack Clubbers, limited to certain models.

A daily limit was added because a few hack clubbers were using 80% of the balance :(
Credits are bought from OpenRouter.

## <Emoji name="brave"/> [Hack Club Search](https://search.hackclub.com/)
Slack channel: [#hackclub-search](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C0A2XCJL6JU)

Free Brave Search API results for Hack Club members

## <Emoji name="blueprint"/> [Workshops](https://workshops.hackclub.com/)
Hack Club has, like, *so* many workshops. A workshop is essentially a mini-lesson that teaches you something new about webdev (or other subjects).
They're bite-sized and *super* digestable (each one is 5-15 minutes). They are workshops for making a snake game, or a stopwatch, or a Chrome extention, or for Python, or React and so. much. more.
Seriously - check it out if you're a beginner to programming or you're looking to learn something new.

# Misc
## <Emoji name="hcb-dark"/> HCB
Hoo boy! HCB! 

HCB is a fiscal sponsor, meaning that it provides its 501(c)(3) tax-exempt status to projects that receive donations through HCB. Legal tax evasion!!! 
When a donation is made to a project through HCB, a 7% fee is taken <Emoji name="sobspin"/> but there are no other fees afterwards! 🎉

Since 2018, HCB has processed 80+ million dollars in transactions across 6500+ projects.
HCB does a lot of stuff for your organization, like...
- automatically file taxes
- pay team members automatically
- accept donations
- issue virtual or physical cards to spend money
- make finances transparent

...and other features!
Learn more at the [official website](https://hackclub.com/fiscal-sponsorship)!

## Subdomain on dino.icu
You can get a subdomain on dino.icu for personal purposes like a portfolio or a random project you made.
You get one by merging a PR into https://github.com/hackclub/dns into the `dino.icu.yaml` file. 
More detailed instructions are on the repo and inside the `dino.icu.yaml` file.
