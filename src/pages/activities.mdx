# Special Activities Division
The Special Activities Division (SAD) hosts all the activities like Gartic phone and Blooket and [Jackbox games](https://www.jackboxgames.com/) and Minecraft Murder Mystery and movie nights and Geoguessr and Chess and [insert a million other activities here]

Yeah...they do *a lot*.
You can see all the past events [here](https://events.hackclub.com/past) if you want.
Here are some activities I wanted to take note of...

## Hackentine
Every year on Valentine's Day, the Special Activities Division hosts Hackentine - an event where Hack Clubbers can send anonymous (or not) platonic messages to other Hack Clubbers. 
Messages are reviewed by the Special Activities Division to make sure that they're appropriate and sent via a DM from a bot.

## AMAs (Ask-me-anything)
Every so often, Hack Club hosts an AMA with a prominent figure in the tech industry. In the past, they've done an AMA with Elon Musk and Daniel Roe 
AMAs are listed on the [past events page](https://events.hackclub.com/past) and also on the [AMA page](https://hackclub.com/amas/)

Sadly, not all AMAs are recorded but the ones that are will (eventually) be uploaded to the [Hack Club YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@HackClubHQ).
Oh, here's a YouTube video that hypes up AMAs on the YouTube channel if you want to watch it: https://youtu.be/O1J1pwGPQXY

# Minecraft
This isn't directly a part of the Special Activities Division, but I still count it as an activity.

Hack Club has its own Minecraft server with public farms and shops and frigging *highways* and a whole bunch of other sillies. 
You will need a Minecraft client that isn't pirated, but there's crossplay between Java and Bedrock (so either client will do). 
The server has QoL mods that prevent your inventory from despawning when you die using graves and letting admins toggle PvP and letting people lock chests.

Join the [#minecraft](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/CD1JSG9UK) channel!
