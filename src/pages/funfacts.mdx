## Shroud
Shroud is used to let Hack Clubbers report concerns anonymously (or not) to the Fire Department.

Shroud's source code is open-source and on GitHub! (https://github.com/hackclub/shroud)

### What is Shrould/being "Shrouded?"
Shroud is the bot thats used to submit anonymous reports about bad behavior from users.
Being "Shrouded" is verb for when someone has been reported to the Fire Department!
